# Getting Started — Install Your First AI Skill in 2 Minutes

This guide gets one **AI automation skill** running in **Claude**, **ChatGPT**, or **Perplexity**. No coding required — if you can download a file and click "upload," you can do this.

It accompanies the book *[AI for Everyday Automation](https://www.amazon.com/AI-Everyday-Automation-Workflows-Hours/dp/1808089812/)* by [Dipankar Sarkar](https://www.dipankar.name).

## Step 1 — Download a skill

Each skill is a small ZIP file in the [`dist/`](../dist) folder. Pick the one that matches your biggest time sink:

| If you spend too much time on... | Download |
|---|---|
| Email | `03-inbox-zero.zip` |
| Meeting prep and follow-up | `04-meetings.zip` |
| Research | `05-research.zip` |
| Writing reports and documents | `06-first-drafts.zip` |
| Tracking tasks | `07-task-extraction.zip` |
| Spreadsheets and data | `08-data-analysis.zip` |
| LinkedIn and announcements | `09-content-on-demand.zip` |
| **Everything** | `all-skills.zip` |

Not sure where to start? Download `01-audit-arm-go.zip` — it helps you decide what to automate first.

## Step 2 — Upload to your AI tool

**Claude (recommended)**
1. Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. **Customize** (left sidebar) → **Skills**
3. **+** → **Upload a skill** → select the ZIP
4. Turn the skill toggle **on**

**ChatGPT**
1. Go to [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com)
2. Profile icon → **Skills** → **New skill** → **Upload from your computer**
3. Select the ZIP

**Perplexity**
1. Go to [perplexity.ai](https://perplexity.ai)
2. **Computer Skills** → upload the ZIP

## Step 3 — Connect your work tools (one-time)

Most skills work best when your AI tool can read your email, calendar, and documents directly. In Claude: **Customize → Integrations**.

| Connect | Unlocks |
|---|---|
| **Gmail** | Inbox Zero (03) |
| **Google Calendar** | Meetings (04) |
| **Google Drive** | Research (05), First Drafts (06), Data Analysis (08) |
| **Task tool** (Asana / Linear / Notion) | Task Extraction (07) |

> No integrations allowed at work? Every skill also runs in **manual mode** — you copy-paste the email or notes instead. The instructions are identical.

## Step 4 — Just talk

The skills trigger automatically from natural language:

- *"Good morning"* → calendar, email, and task briefing
- *"Check my email"* → triage inbox, draft replies
- *"Here's my meeting transcript"* → summary, tasks, follow-up email
- *"Research the market for X"* → sourced brief
- *"Write my weekly status report"* → finished draft saved to Drive

You approve everything before it is sent.
