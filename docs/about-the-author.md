# About the Author — Dipankar Sarkar

**Website:** [dipankar.name](https://www.dipankar.name)
**Book:** *[AI for Everyday Automation](https://www.amazon.com/AI-Everyday-Automation-Workflows-Hours/dp/1808089812/)* (Packt Publishing)

[Dipankar Sarkar](https://www.dipankar.name) is an engineering leader and AI architect with 18+ years of experience building scalable systems and high-performing teams, currently focused on AI engineering and production systems for regulated industries.

## Background

- Founder at **Neul Labs**
- **Principal AI Architect** at a UK fintech (financial services)
- Former leadership roles at **Nykaa**, **Hike**, **Orangewood Labs**, and **SlideShare**
- Has led platforms serving **250M+ users**
- **60+ patents** filed across his work and research initiatives

## Education

- **B.Tech in Computer Science**, IIT Delhi
- **M.S. in Computer Science**, Arizona State University

## Writing

Dipankar writes about applied AI, automation, and engineering leadership. *AI for Everyday Automation* distils years of building production AI systems into practical workflows anyone can use to save hours every week.

Find more of his work at **[dipankar.name](https://www.dipankar.name)**.
