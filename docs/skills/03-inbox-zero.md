# Inbox Zero — AI Email Triage and Reply Drafting

**Book chapter:** 3 · **Download:** [`03-inbox-zero.zip`](../../dist/03-inbox-zero.zip) · **Source:** [`SKILL.md`](../../skills/03-inbox-zero/SKILL.md)

## What this skill does

Inbox Zero processes your entire inbox in one connected session. It reads unread Gmail, **classifies each email by urgency** (act now / act later / inform only), and **drafts replies in your voice** for the urgent ones — then waits for your approval before anything is sent. Use it for email triage, follow-ups, declining meetings, handling difficult emails, and batch email.

## Say something like

- *"Check my email"*
- *"Process my inbox"*
- *"Draft a reply"*
- *"Decline this meeting"*
- *"Follow up on this deliverable"*
- *"Handle this complaint"*

## Integrations required

**Gmail** (or run in manual mode by pasting emails into the conversation).
