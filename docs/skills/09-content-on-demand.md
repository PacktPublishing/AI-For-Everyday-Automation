# Content on Demand — AI LinkedIn Posts, Announcements, and Updates

**Book chapter:** 9 · **Download:** [`09-content-on-demand.zip`](../../dist/09-content-on-demand.zip) · **Source:** [`SKILL.md`](../../skills/09-content-on-demand/SKILL.md)

## What this skill does

This skill turns a single update into professional content across formats — **LinkedIn posts, internal announcements, client updates, newsletter sections, presentation outlines, launch announcements, and press releases** — and repurposes one piece into many channels.

## Say something like

- *"Write a LinkedIn post about this"*
- *"Draft an internal announcement"*
- *"Write a client update"*
- *"Repurpose this for our newsletter"*
- *"Launch announcement for..."*

## Integrations required

**Google Drive** and **Gmail**. Manual mode also supported.

## Part of a system

This is skill 09 of 10 in **AI For Everyday Automation**. It turns status reports from [First Drafts (06)](06-first-drafts.md) and charts from [Data Analysis (08)](08-data-analysis.md) into published content, and is part of [Your Automated Week (10)](10-automated-week.md).
