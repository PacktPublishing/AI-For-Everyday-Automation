# Meetings That Actually Work — AI Briefs, Summaries, and Action Items

**Book chapter:** 4 · **Download:** [`04-meetings.zip`](../../dist/04-meetings.zip) · **Source:** [`SKILL.md`](../../skills/04-meetings/SKILL.md)

## What this skill does

This skill runs your full meeting workflow with AI: **pre-meeting briefs**, **morning calendar briefings**, **meeting summaries and packets** from a transcript, **action-item extraction**, and **task creation** — including recurring meeting automation. Turn any meeting transcript into a summary, a task list, and a follow-up email.

## Say something like

- *"What meetings do I have today?"*
- *"Prepare for my meeting"*
- *"Process this transcript"*
- *"Meeting packet"*
- *"Action items from this meeting"*
- *"Brief me for today"*

## Integrations required

**Google Calendar, Google Drive, Gmail, and a task tool** (Asana / Linear / Notion). Manual mode also supported.

## Part of a system

This is skill 04 of 10 in **AI For Everyday Automation**. Meeting packets feed [First Drafts (06)](06-first-drafts.md), action items feed [Task Extraction (07)](07-task-extraction.md), and it anchors [Your Automated Week (10)](10-automated-week.md).
