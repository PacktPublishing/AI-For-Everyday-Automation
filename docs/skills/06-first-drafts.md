# First Drafts in 15 Minutes — AI Report and Document Writing

**Book chapter:** 6 · **Download:** [`06-first-drafts.zip`](../../dist/06-first-drafts.zip) · **Source:** [`SKILL.md`](../../skills/06-first-drafts/SKILL.md)

## What this skill does

This skill turns raw notes, meeting packets, and research into a **polished first draft** in about 15 minutes — reports, proposals, memos, status updates, and client summaries. It can run a full document-generation chain and save the result to Google Drive.

## Say something like

- *"Write my weekly status report"*
- *"Draft a proposal for..."*
- *"Turn these notes into a memo"*
- *"Write a client summary"*
- *"First draft of..."*

## Integrations required

**Google Drive** and **Gmail**, and optionally a task tool. Manual mode also supported.

## Part of a system

This is skill 06 of 10 in **AI For Everyday Automation**. It consumes meeting packets from [Meetings (04)](04-meetings.md), research from [Research (05)](05-research.md), and task data from [Task Extraction (07)](07-task-extraction.md), then feeds [Content on Demand (09)](09-content-on-demand.md).
