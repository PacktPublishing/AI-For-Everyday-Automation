# Research in 30 Minutes — AI Research Briefs With Citations

**Book chapter:** 5 · **Download:** [`05-research.zip`](../../dist/05-research.zip) · **Source:** [`SKILL.md`](../../skills/05-research/SKILL.md)

## What this skill does

This skill produces **sourced research briefs with citations** in about 30 minutes. Use it to research a topic, compare options, analyse competitors, investigate a problem, or understand trends — combining web search with your own documents in Google Drive.

## Say something like

- *"Research the competitive landscape for X"*
- *"Compare these options"*
- *"What are the current trends in...?"*
- *"Help me decide between A and B"*
- *"Give me a sourced brief on..."*

## Integrations required

**Web search**, and optionally **Google Drive** for your own source documents.

This is skill 05 of 10 in **AI For Everyday Automation**. Research briefs become the backbone of proposals and reports in [First Drafts (06)](06-first-drafts.md), and feed [Your Automated Week (10)](10-automated-week.md).
