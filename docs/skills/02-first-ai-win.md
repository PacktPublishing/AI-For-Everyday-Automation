## What this skill does

Your First AI Win is the introductory skill for first-time users. It gives you a **guided walkthrough of your first AI-assisted task** — drafting emails, summarising a meeting, or turning rough notes into a memo — so you get a real, useful result on day one.

## Say something like

- *"Help me try AI on a real task"*
- *"First time using AI for work"*
- *"Draft some emails for practice"*
- *"Summarise this meeting for me"*
- *"Turn these notes into a memo"*

## Integrations required

None — runs in any AI tool.
