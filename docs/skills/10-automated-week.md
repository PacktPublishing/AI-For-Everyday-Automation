## What this skill does

This is the capstone skill that runs your **complete weekly AI system**: the Monday morning launch, the daily morning prompt, and the Friday close — calendar, email, tasks, meeting briefs, status sweep, and next-week prep. Just say *"good morning"* and the whole routine fires.

## Say something like

- *"Good morning"*
- *"Start my day"*
- *"Monday launch"*
- *"Friday close"*
- *"What should I focus on today?"*
- *"Run my morning routine"*

## Integrations required

**Google Calendar, Gmail, Google Drive, and a task tool** — it ties all the other skills together.

## Part of a system

This is skill 10 of 10 in **AI For Everyday Automation**. It orchestrates [Inbox Zero (03)](03-inbox-zero.md), [Meetings (04)](04-meetings.md), [Research (05)](05-research.md), [First Drafts (06)](06-first-drafts.md), [Task Extraction (07)](07-task-extraction.md), [Data Analysis (08)](08-data-analysis.md), and [Content on Demand (09)](09-content-on-demand.md) into one Monday-to-Friday rhythm.
